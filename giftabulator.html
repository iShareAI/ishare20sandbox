Executive Summary

Giftabulator® is a donor engagement and gift planning tool that instantly visualizes tax-efficient donation scenarios, empowering donors to maximize their charitable impact while optimizing financial outcomes. By making complex planned giving strategies more transparent and accessible, it transforms conversations between charities, advisors, and donors.

The core challenge is that traditional planned giving conversations are often opaque, time-consuming, and dependent on specialized expertise. Donors struggle to understand tax benefits, charities lack tools to illustrate scenarios in real time, and financial advisors need efficient ways to integrate philanthropy into wealth planning discussions.

Giftabulator® solves this by providing immediate, interactive visualization of giving scenarios using stocks, mutual funds, real estate, life insurance, RRSP/RRIF assets, and other vehicles. The result is a more confident donor, a better fundraising conversation, and a stronger pathway to significantly larger gifts.

The Problem

Traditional planned giving remains inaccessible and inefficient for the three groups that matter most: charities, advisors, and donors.

01

For Charities

Most donors continue giving cash simply because they do not understand the more compelling alternatives. Planned giving options remain mysterious to many supporters, and fundraisers often lack tools to model scenarios live during donor conversations.

  • Donor knowledge gaps limit larger gifts.
  • Static follow-up materials often fail to convert interest into action.
  • Organizations leave significant revenue unrealized when asset-based giving is not explained clearly.
02

For Financial Advisors

Advisors want to integrate philanthropy into holistic wealth planning, but charitable planning is often too slow, too technical, and too difficult to illustrate in real time.

  • Planned giving calculations can be time-intensive.
  • Clients rarely initiate philanthropic discussions on their own.
  • Without strong visualization tools, philanthropy stays secondary instead of strategic.
03

For Donors

Donors often face decision paralysis. They do not know which assets to donate, how different giving vehicles compare, or how to align generosity with financial efficiency.

  • Complex technical language creates unnecessary barriers.
  • Traditional planned giving materials can feel intimidating rather than inspiring.
  • Many donors give less than they could because the pathway to smart giving is unclear.

The Solution

Giftabulator® makes smart giving visible, immediate, and easier to act on.

Giftabulator® turns planned giving from a difficult, delayed conversation into an interactive decision tool that works in real time.

1

Real-Time Asset Donation Scenarios

Users can input asset type, value, adjusted cost base, province or territory, income level, and desired gift amount. The platform instantly calculates charitable tax credit, tax savings, net gift cost, cash comparisons, and capital gains tax avoided.

2

Endowment Growth Visualization

Giftabulator® helps donors see how a gift may grow over time, what annual disbursement it can support, and how it may create long-term legacy impact through endowment funding and naming opportunities.

3

Customizable Branding

Charities can tailor the experience with their own identity, giving priorities, impact stories, recognition thresholds, and QR-code access for meetings, events, and campaigns.

4

Instant Report Generation

After exploring scenarios, users can generate PDF reports showing methodology, side-by-side options, next steps, and contact details for charity or advisor follow-up.

5

iShare® Integration

When connected to the iShare® ecosystem, Giftabulator® becomes part of a larger donor journey that blends financial clarity, immersive storytelling, AI-driven personalization, and stronger donor motivation.

What Donors Can See

A donor comparing a cash gift with an appreciated stock donation can immediately see the difference in charitable impact, tax savings, and net out-of-pocket cost, making generosity feel more achievable.

Implementation Considerations

Success depends on more than deployment. It depends on how well the tool is integrated into donor communication, staff confidence, and organizational process.

Privacy and Security

Giftabulator® can operate with donor anonymity by design. Users can explore scenarios without immediately sharing personal information, and organizations can introduce the tool without increasing friction at the start of the journey.

Training and Change Management

Development staff need confidence in how to introduce the tool: not as a calculator alone, but as a way to explore options together. Advisor partnerships and impact storytelling should reinforce, not compete with, the financial conversation.

Measurement

Track usage, scenario completion, conversion to commitment, average gift size, share of asset-based gifts, pipeline growth, and staff confidence to understand both financial and operational return.

Pricing and Investment
