Campus of Care

A calm, green courtyard designed for reflection and connection. Surrounded by light-filled spaces, the Campus of Care brings people together in a natural setting that promotes well-being and community.

Garden Courtyard at the Heart

A sunlit inner garden with looping paths and seasonal planting offers places to sit, stroll, and gather, connecting all wings of the campus and bringing nature into daily routines.

Designed for Families

Soft finishes, clear wayfinding, and abundant daylight support dignified, unhurried daily life for residents and loved ones.

Modern Facade with Character

Vertical panels, bright glazing, and open balconies create a welcoming identity. Soft landscaping and clear pathways connect the building with a sense of openness and care.
