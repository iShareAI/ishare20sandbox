Marshall McLuhan was right
The medium really is the message when it comes to effective communication. Charities striving to describe their needs and steward their mission need the right messaging tools to cultivate lasting and mutually beneficial connections with their donors.

Harnessing visualization and stakeholder engagement through Virtual Reality
Our call to action urges charities to leverage the power of Virtual Reality through smart stakeholder engagement as transformative tools in their communication, marketing, and stewardship activities.

iShare®'s intuitive Virtual Reality platform activates engagement with stakeholders which is key to building trust, fostering transparency, and nurturing long-term relationships with supporters.
