About the Project

A curated 360 walkthrough designed to showcase NBCC's key spaces, spatial logic, and stakeholder journey in a fully immersive environment.

Opportunity Gift Level Description
Welcome Zone $750,000 Naming Opportunity The Welcome Zone is the front door to the Genomics building, a place where students, researchers, partners, and visitors first encounter the promise of genomic science. It sets the tone for the entire facility, orienting guests, showcasing stories of discovery, and highlighting how genomics is reshaping medicine, agriculture, and society.

A named Welcome Zone creates an immediate association between the donor and the future of genomics, signaling hospitality, accessibility, and a commitment to inviting the broader community into cutting-edge science. It becomes the gathering point for tours, outreach events, and milestone celebrations that connect genomics to real-world impact.
Core Learning Space $500,000 Naming Opportunity The Core Learning Space is where the next generation of scientists and health professionals build their foundational understanding of genomics. Designed for active, experiential learning, it supports everything from introductory courses to advanced, research-based projects that let students contribute to real datasets and discovery.

As genomics becomes central to biology, medicine, and public health, this space equips learners with the literacy and practical skills they need to thrive in a data-rich, rapidly evolving field. A named Core Learning Space affirms a donor's belief that every student should have access to high-quality genomics education, not just a select few.
Collaboration Area $250,000 Naming Opportunity Breakthroughs in genomics rarely happen in isolation. The Collaboration Area is intentionally designed as an "in-between" space where faculty, trainees, data scientists, clinicians, and partners can meet informally, share ideas, and spark new interdisciplinary projects.

With flexible seating, writable surfaces, and visual access to labs and learning spaces, this area encourages the kind of spontaneous conversations that lead to new questions, grants, and discoveries. A named Collaboration Area positions the donor at the heart of a vibrant intellectual community, where proximity and design actively accelerate scientific progress.
Future Expansion Zone $1 Million Naming Opportunity The Future Expansion Zone is a strategic reserve of space that ensures the Genomics building can respond to new technologies, programs, and partnerships as the field continues its rapid growth. With global genomics activity and investment projected to expand significantly in the coming years, planning for future capacity is critical.

This zone will allow the School to add new platforms, such as population genomics initiatives, advanced bioinformatics suites, or specialized training programs, without disrupting existing activity. A named Future Expansion Zone signals a donor's vision and long-term commitment, ensuring that the facility can adapt and lead as genomics evolves in ways we can only begin to imagine today.
