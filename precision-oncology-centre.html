The Vision

Cancer is not one disease. It is thousands. For too long, treatment followed a broad, generalized model. The Precision Oncology Centre signals the end of that era.

Here, every diagnosis is analyzed at the cellular level. Every treatment plan is shaped around the biology of the individual. Every decision is informed by genomic sequencing, molecular profiling, multidisciplinary expertise, and advanced treatment planning.

A transformative $10 million naming gift will help establish the Centre as a national leader in personalized cancer care, connecting advanced science with patient-centred healing and long-term discovery.
